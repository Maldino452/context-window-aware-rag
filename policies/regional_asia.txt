# Regional Guidelines - Asia - Aurelius Consulting Group

## Transportation
Flights:
- Long distances require flights
- Budget carriers acceptable
- Full-service for long flights (meals, baggage included)

Ground:
- Grab (SE Asia), Didi (China)
- Metered taxis (ensure meter running)
- Excellent public transport in major cities
- Private drivers acceptable for multiple meetings

Trains:
- High-speed rail in China, Japan, Korea
- First class over 3 hours

## Accommodation Rates
- Tokyo, Singapore, Hong Kong: $220/night
- Shanghai, Beijing, Seoul: $180/night
- Mumbai, Bangkok, Manila: $150/night
- Secondary cities: $130/night

## Meal Allowances
- Japan, Singapore, Hong Kong: $90/day
- China, Korea, Taiwan: $75/day
- India: $65/day
- Southeast Asia: $65/day

## Business Culture
- Business cards: present with both hands
- Punctuality critical
- Remove shoes in some settings
- Respect hierarchy
- Gift-giving customs vary
