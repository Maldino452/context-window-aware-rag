# Regional Guidelines - Europe - Aurelius Consulting Group

## Transportation
Trains vs Flights:
- Under 400km: trains preferred
- Paris-London, Amsterdam-Brussels: high-speed rail
- First class rail allowed over 3 hours

Intra-Europe flights:
- Economy only
- Budget airlines acceptable (Ryanair, EasyJet)
- Consider baggage fees

## Accommodation Rates
- Major cities (London, Paris, Zurich): €200/night
- Secondary cities: €150/night
- Scandinavia: €220/night (higher cost)
- Eastern Europe: €120/night

## Meal Allowances
- UK, Switzerland, Scandinavia: €70/day
- France, Germany, Netherlands: €65/day
- Spain, Italy, Portugal: €60/day
- Eastern Europe: €50/day

## Business Culture
- Lunch break: 12:00-2:00pm
- Shops may close midday (Spain, Italy)
- Sunday: most businesses closed
- English widely spoken in business
