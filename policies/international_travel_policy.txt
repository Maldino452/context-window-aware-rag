# International Travel Policy - Aurelius Consulting Group

## Pre-Approval
International travel requires:
- Manager approval 30 days advance
- Travel risk assessment
- Client confirmation
- Budget estimate

## Travel Documentation
Ensure you have:
- Valid passport (6+ months validity)
- Required visas (employee responsibility)
- Travel insurance (auto-provided)
- Emergency contacts

## Visa Costs
Reimbursable:
- Visa application fees
- Expedited processing (urgent travel)
- Passport fees (for business travel)

## Per Diem by Region

Europe:
- UK, France, Germany, Switzerland: €70/day
- Spain, Italy, Eastern Europe: €60/day
- Scandinavia: €80/day

Asia:
- Japan, Singapore, Hong Kong: $90/day
- China, Korea, Taiwan: $75/day
- India, Southeast Asia: $65/day

Middle East: $85/day
Americas: $70-80/day
Africa: $65-75/day
Australia/NZ: AUD $95/day

## Currency Exchange
- Use company credit card (best rates)
- ATM withdrawals reimbursable (once per trip)
- ATM fees up to $10
- Keep receipts in original currency
