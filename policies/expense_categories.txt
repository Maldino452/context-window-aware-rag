# Expense Categories and Codes - Aurelius Consulting Group

## Primary Categories

### Transportation
- AIR: Airfare
- RAIL: Train tickets
- RIDE: Ride-hailing (Uber, Lyft, taxi)
- PUBLIC: Public transportation
- RENTAL: Car rental
- PARKING: Parking fees
- MILEAGE: Personal vehicle

### Accommodation
- HOTEL: Hotel charges
- LODGING: Alternative accommodation

### Meals
- BREAKFAST: Morning meals
- LUNCH: Midday meals
- DINNER: Evening meals
- ENTERTAIN: Client entertainment

### Other
- BAGGAGE: Luggage fees
- VISA: Visa and passport
- COMM: Communication (phone, data)
- MISC: Other business expenses

## Expense Limits Quick Reference

Domestic meals per day: $80
International meals: $65-90/day
Taxi tips: Up to 20%
Lost receipt max: $75
Hotel domestic Tier 1: $250/night
Checked bag: 3+ day trips only
Flight economy: Under 6 hours
Manager approval: Exceptions under $500
