# Flight Policy - Aurelius Consulting Group

Flights may be claimed for approved business travel.

## Allowed Classes
- Economy class for flights under 6 hours (mandatory)
- Premium economy for flights 6-10 hours  
- Business class for flights over 10 hours (requires director approval)

## Booking Requirements
- Flights should be booked at least 7 days in advance
- Urgent travel exceptions allowed with documentation
- Use TravelHub portal unless vendor unavailable

## Route Selection
Select most direct route available. Indirect routes requiring justification if more than 2 hours longer.

## Seat Selection
- Standard seat selection reimbursable
- Extra legroom reimbursable for flights over 4 hours
- Paid upgrades at check-in not reimbursable
- Loyalty redemptions are personal benefit (non-reimbursable)

## Baggage
See separate luggage policy document.

## Flight Changes
Change fees reimbursable if:
- Client meeting rescheduled
- Business needs changed
- Weather or airline issues
- Medical emergency (documentation required)

Personal reasons for changes are not reimbursable.
