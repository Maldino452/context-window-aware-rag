# Reimbursement Process - Aurelius Consulting Group

## Timeline
Submit within 30 days of trip completion.

Late submissions (30-60 days):
- Require manager approval
- Subject to review

Over 60 days:
- Will not be reimbursed
- Rare exceptions with VP approval

## Submission Steps
1. Gather all receipts and approvals
2. Log into ExpenseHub portal
3. Select "New Expense Report"  
4. Enter trip details and expenses
5. Upload receipts
6. Submit to manager

## Approval Workflow
1. Manager review (1-2 days)
2. Finance review (1-2 days)
3. Payment processing (3-5 days)

Total: 7-10 business days

## Payment Methods
- Direct deposit (standard)
- PayPal (if on file)
- Check (10-15 days)

## Rejection Reasons
- Missing receipts
- Outside policy without approval
- Personal expenses
- Insufficient justification
- Duplicates
