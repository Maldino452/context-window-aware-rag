# Luggage Policy - Aurelius Consulting Group

## Checked Baggage
Checked baggage fees reimbursable only if:

- Required for business equipment, OR
- Trip duration exceeds 3 days

For trips under 3 days: carry-on sufficient (not reimbursable)

## Carry-On Baggage
Carry-on fees are ALWAYS reimbursable.

## Excess or Overweight Baggage
NOT reimbursable unless pre-approved for business equipment.

## Lost or Delayed Baggage
If baggage delayed/lost:

First 24 hours:
- Essentials: toiletries, basic clothing
- Maximum $150
- Keep receipts

Extended delay (24-48 hours):
- Additional clothing up to $300 total
- Business attire priority

Lost baggage:
- File airline claim first
- Company insurance covers up to $1,500
- Personal items not reimbursable
