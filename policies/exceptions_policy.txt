# Exceptions and Approvals - Aurelius Consulting Group

Exceptions to standard policies may be granted under exceptional circumstances.

## All Exceptions Require
- Written manager approval (BEFORE incurring expense)
- Submission of detailed justification
- Estimate of additional cost

## Approval Levels
- Up to $500 over policy: Manager approval
- $500-$2000 over policy: Director approval  
- Over $2000: VP approval required

## Common Exception Scenarios
- Conference hotels exceeding rate limits
- Medical conditions requiring upgraded seats
- High cost-of-living cities
- Last-minute urgent travel
- Group travel with client executives

## Without Approval
Expenses outside policy will be rejected.

## Retroactive Requests
Exceptions requested AFTER expense incurred:
- Less likely to be approved
- Require extraordinary justification
- VP approval required regardless of amount
