# Receipt Policy - Aurelius Consulting Group

## When Receipts Are Mandatory
ALWAYS required for:
- Any expense over $25
- All accommodation
- All flights
- Rental cars
- Client entertainment (any amount)
- Alcohol (any amount)

## When Optional
- Public transport under $25
- Parking under $10
- Tips under $20
- Meals under $25 (within per diem)

## Valid Receipts Must Include
- Vendor name and location
- Date of transaction
- Itemized list
- Total amount
- Payment method

## Lost Receipt Protocol
- Submit with explanation
- Provide credit card statement
- Maximum $75 per lost receipt
- Maximum 3 lost receipts per quarter
- Manager approval required
