# Ground Transport Policy - Aurelius Consulting Group

Employees may claim ground transport during business travel.

## Allowed Transport Methods

### Ride-Hailing Services
- Standard service only (UberX, Lyft Standard)
- Premium services require justification:
  * Groups of 4+ employees
  * Excessive luggage
  * Standard service unavailable

### Taxis  
- Allowed when ride-hailing unavailable
- Must provide itemized receipt with date, locations, fare
- Tips up to 20% reimbursable

### Public Transportation
- Always preferred when practical
- All public transport reimbursable: trains, metros, buses
- No receipt required for trips under $25
- Weekly/monthly passes reimbursable with receipt

### Rental Cars
Require manager pre-approval. Only authorized when:
- Multiple meetings at different locations same day
- Public transport unavailable
- Group of 3+ employees

Guidelines:
- Compact or mid-size vehicles only
- Full insurance coverage mandatory
- Fuel receipts required
- Return with full tank
- Parking and toll receipts required

### Personal Vehicle
- Reimbursed at $0.58 per mile
- Must log: date, destination, purpose, odometer readings
- NOT for regular office commute
- Parking and tolls reimbursable with receipts

## Non-Reimbursable
- Luxury vehicles without justification
- Personal use during business trips
- Traffic fines, parking tickets
- Vehicle damage
- Personal errands
