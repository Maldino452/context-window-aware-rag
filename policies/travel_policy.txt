# Travel Policy - Aurelius Consulting Group

All employees may claim expenses for approved business travel.

## What Qualifies as Business Travel
- Client site visits and engagement work
- Internal company events and meetings  
- Approved training programs and conferences
- Recruitment and interviewing activities

## Pre-Approval Requirements
All travel must be pre-approved by direct manager before booking.

Exception: Emergency client situations requiring same-day travel.

Pre-approval requires:
- Travel dates and destination
- Business justification
- Estimated total cost
- Client code or cost center

## Booking Requirements
Travel must be booked using approved vendors:
- Flights: TravelHub portal
- Hotels: TravelHub portal or direct booking
- Ground transport: Direct booking with receipts

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses
Personal travel expenses are not reimbursable:
- Personal vacation added to business trips
- Family member travel
- Entertainment unrelated to business
- Minibar and in-room movies
- Spa services
- Personal shopping
