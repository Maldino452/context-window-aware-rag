# Cancellation Policy - Aurelius Consulting Group

Flight and accommodation cancellation fees are reimbursable if:

## Business Reasons
- Client meeting cancelled/rescheduled
- Project changed or cancelled
- Manager direction to cancel

## Medical Reasons
- Employee illness or injury (documentation required)
- Family medical emergency

## Other Covered Reasons
- Severe weather or natural disaster
- Airline/hotel operational issues
- Government travel restrictions

## NOT Reimbursable
Cancellations for personal reasons:
- Personal vacation conflicts
- Family events (non-emergency)
- Preference changes

## Documentation Required
- Original booking confirmation
- Cancellation confirmation
- Cancellation fee breakdown
- Business justification email
