# Accommodation Policy - Aurelius Consulting Group

## Nightly Rate Limits
Maximum rates (excluding taxes):

Domestic:
- Tier 1 cities (major metros): $250/night
- Tier 2 cities (regional): $180/night  
- Tier 3 cities (small markets): $150/night

International:
- Europe major cities: €200/night
- Europe other: €150/night
- Asia major cities: $220/night
- Asia other: $160/night

## Rate Exceptions
Rates above limits require manager approval with:
- No available hotels within limit, OR
- Conference block rate, OR
- Safety considerations

## Hotel Selection
Choose hotels that:
- Are within 30 minutes of client site
- Offer business amenities (WiFi, workspace)
- Meet safety standards
- Provide breakfast when available

## Room Type
Standard single occupancy only. Suites not reimbursable unless:
- Standard rooms unavailable
- Extended stay over 7 nights

## Reimbursable Amenities
- Standard WiFi
- Business center
- Hotel breakfast (if not included)
- Reasonable parking

## NOT Reimbursable
- In-room movies
- Minibar charges
- Spa or gym fees
- Room service (except late arrival)
- Late checkout fees
- Laundry (unless trip over 5 days)
